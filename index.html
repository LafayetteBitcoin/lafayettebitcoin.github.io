Our Mission

To bring awareness of the versatility of BITCOIN and its practically for use in the common market place. We wish to educate the community on the cryptocurrency and the advantages and risks of its use. The concept of digital money can be a hard concept to grasp at times. We want to enable the the exchange of ideas, current affairs, as well as local trade of goods, services, and commodities. Moreover, we lead our area to understand the signifigance of the The Blockchain technologies.
